Finhazi SACCO Management Software for Africa

Finhazi is a cloud-based SACCO management system and core banking platform built specifically for African cooperatives, SACCOs, VSLAs, youth funds, and microfinance institutions. It helps you digitize savings, loans, contributions, and member accounts in one secure, easy-to-use system.

Why SACCOs Need Digital Core Banking

Many SACCOs and savings groups still rely on paper ledgers and spreadsheets. This creates problems with transparency, reconciliation, compliance, and member trust. Finhazi replaces manual processes with a modern digital platform that:

  • Centralizes member, savings, and loan records
  • Automates interest, penalties, and repayment schedules
  • Improves transparency and accountability for committees and auditors
  • Provides members with clear statements and balances
  • Enables mobile, USSD, and SMS access where supported

Why Finhazi is One of the Best SACCO Software Platforms in Africa

Finhazi is designed in Africa for African realities. Whether you are a community SACCO, a large cooperative, or a VSLA network, Finhazi gives you the tools to operate like a modern financial institution without the complexity and cost of traditional core banking systems.

Key SACCO Features

  • Digital Member Management: Onboard members, manage KYC details, and track membership status.
  • Savings & Shares: Record deposits, shares, and contributions across multiple products.
  • Loans & Credit: Configure loan products, terms, and interest; track disbursements and repayments.
  • VSLA & Group Features: Support rotating savings, group contributions, fines, and distributions.
  • Core Banking Ledger: Maintain account balances with a robust financial backbone.
  • Statements & Reporting: Generate reports for management, regulators, and auditors.
  • Payment Integrations: Connect to mobile money and bank channels where available.
